About Us - Paddle And Peak

Our company is dedicated to providing thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you.

Our History

Founded with a passion for adventure, Paddle And Peak Adventures has been guiding rafters for decades. Our journey started with a few friends who loved the outdoors and grew into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region.

From small rivers to major rapids, our story is one of courage, teamwork, and unforgettable fun. Our mission is to connect people with nature while keeping safety and sustainability at the forefront.
